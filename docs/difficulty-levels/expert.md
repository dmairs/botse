This is the hardest difficulty.

[Adventurers](/docs/glossary/adventurer) gain 2 [fatigue](/docs/glossary/fatigue) for each additional [engage](/docs/glossary/engage) action taken in battle. To resolve their individual [Adventurers Rest](/docs/day/end-of-day-phase) step, an adventurer must [discard](/docs/glossary/discard) an [item](/docs/glossary/item). Adventurers remove 1 die from their cooldown track and [heal](/docs/glossary/healing) for 1 HP during the Adventurers Rest step at the end of each day.
